Currency System

  • Coins

    Earned by defeating monsters. Used to buy items from the trader and can be exchanged for Clovers.

  • Clovers

    A rare currency that can sometimes be obtained from monsters. Pets with good luck may also bring you more. Used in summoning to obtain Xylo, Coins, Stardust, and more.

  • Xylo

    Used to summon legends (requires 3 Xylo per summon). It can only be obtained by summoning with Clovers.

  • Gold

    Obtained every 5 levels and can be used to summon legends. Also given during giveaways and events.

  • Stardust

    The rarest summoning item, used to upgrade legends by enhancing their stats and abilities. It can only be obtained through summons.

Hunting System

You can hunt monsters using the /hunt command. Each monster has unique item drops that can be used for crafting. Defeating a monster grants you experience points (EXP) and coins.

PvP System

  • Attack

    Attacking deals damage to your opponent based on your equipped weapon. Each successful attack grants +1 energy, which can be used for resonance.

  • Resonate

    Resonance consumes energy to upgrade your weapon to the next stage, increasing its maximum damage output. Upon reaching the maximum resonance level, some weapons unlock special moves for powerful attacks.

  • Battle Items

    Players can equip up to four types of battle items. These items provide various effects, such as healing, buffs, or debuffs. Battle items can be obtained through gameplay and used strategically during fights.

  • Victory and Defeat

    After winning a battle, the victor can choose one of the following actions:

    1. Steal Coins:

      Take 15-25% of the defeated player's coins.

    2. Steal Item:

      Loot a random item from the loser’s inventory.

    3. Imprison:

      Temporarily lock the losing player out of the game. The imprisoned player can regain access by fulfilling the winner’s demands (such as paying coins or giving up an item).

    4. Spare:

      Let the losing player go without penalty.

Adventure System

Players can go on adventures using the /adventure command, with a 5-minute cooldown between each attempt. During an adventure, they may encounter random events such as merchants, hidden treasures, or special quests. There is also a chance to obtain rare items, weapons, and armor with unique effects. Adventuring is a great way to discover valuable gear and new opportunities.
